I am drawn to mechatronics and power systems, and I like getting hardware and software to work together on the bench. I have also explored cybersecurity, which informs how I think about connected and embedded designs.

I am open to relocating for the right opportunity, especially in the Boston area; I previously studied at Boston University and would welcome being back in that region.

Day-to-day tools: C, Python, MATLAB, LTspice.
